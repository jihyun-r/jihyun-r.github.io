Bio

I am a graduate student (Ph.D.) in Electical Engineering and Computer Science at The Pennsylvania State University. My advisor is Prof. Mahmut Kandemir.

My current research is mainly focused on the architectural design of hardware accelerators for the machine learning algorithms. Recently, I have been working on analyzing and optimizing multiple machine learning and deep learning algorithms for the parallel architectures. Previously, I also worked on handheld system design such as mobile phones or tablets and general IP architectures.
